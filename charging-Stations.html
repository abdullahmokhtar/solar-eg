E-mobility is arriving.

In Switzerland, on the streets, at home. All you need is a suitable charging station. Helion also offers intelligent energy management. So that you can get the most out of your electric car. And you are simply mobile without any worries.

This is what we offer you

We plan and create intelligent charging infrastructure for your home. We use established products from partners – for example our wallboxes – which guarantee you high quality. If you have a solar system or heat pump, we integrate the charging station into your home's energy system, including bi-directional charging. And of course our technical support is available to you even after commissioning.

The mobility of many advantages

Driving and charging electrically offers several advantages Cheaper – The electric car is today the cheapest car in terms of full costs (TCO, Total Cost of Ownership). This is due to the unbeatable efficiency of the electric motor and the falling vehicle costs. More ecological - you can twist and turn it however you want: the electric car is the most environmentally friendly car, even if production is taken into account. Because it is emission-free in operation - the advantage increases with every kilometer driven. New business models – Companies that operate charging infrastructure can either open up new sources of income or give their customers, partner companies and employees an additional benefit. The ideal complement to photovoltaics – if you have a solar system, your electric car is even more environmentally friendly and uses the electricity from your own roof. In addition, the electric car can be integrated into the home energy system as a storage device with bidirectional charging.
